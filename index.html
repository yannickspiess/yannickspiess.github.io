Yannick Spiess
Artist · Filmmaker · Writer
Contact

Yannick Spiess is an artist whose practice spans moving image, writing, and visual composition. His projects bring together formal clarity and emotional tension, often exploring the relationship between memory, technology, and lived experience.

  • The Days
    2018 · 20 min · Experimental documentary
    Portrait of a retired woman in Switzerland that opens into a broader reflection on labor, aging, and social structure.
    Internationale Kurzfilmtage · Oberhausen
  • Pixels in a Landscape
    2016 · 35 min · Science fiction
    Science-fiction work exploring digital perception and identity through intersecting virtual worlds.
    POST MORTEM · Aguascalientes 中国微电影微视频十年大展 · Beijing World Horror Con Film Festival · Salt Lake City
  • The Sound of a Harddrive
    2020 · 14 min · Video essay
    Video essay about online play, digital public space, and social disconnection, shot entirely inside Minecraft.
    European Media Art Festival · Osnabrück
  • No Other Girl
    2016 · 17 min · Fiction
    Fiction relationship drama told through fragmented perspectives and altered causality.
    Equality Festival · Kyiv Thessaloniki International LGBTQ Film Festival · Thessaloniki Berlin Art Film Festival · Berlin
  • Long Distance
    Video series · Found footage
    Series of short artistic films built from found footage, shown in an exhibition with Lagoon Parliament.
    transmediale.berlin · Berlin kunstakademie.berlin · Berlin
  • Antiprinz
    2018 · 16 min · Experimental fiction
    Experimental fiction set inside a real-life avatar game where voyeurism and identity blur.
  • Bauland
    2020 · 18 min · Fiction
    Fiction film following Marcel's return to his place of origin, where clean surfaces reveal a rougher reality.
